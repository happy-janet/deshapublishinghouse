About Desha Publishing House

At Desha Publishing House, we believe in the transformative power of words. Founded on the principles of integrity, creativity, inclusivity, we strive to discover and nurture emerging and established authors, publishing works that inspire, educate, and entertain.

From captivating novels to insightful non-fiction, our diverse catalog reflects our dedication to enriching the literary landscape. We are more than just a publishing house; we are a community of storytellers, editors, and readers united by a shared love of books.

Our Commitment

Desha Publishing House is committed to publishing exceptional books that resonate with readers. We are dedicated to nurturing and supporting our authors, providing them with the resources and expertise they need to bring their stories to life. We uphold the highest standards of quality in every aspect of our work, from editorial excellence to innovative marketing, ensuring that every book we publish makes a meaningful contribution to the literary world.

Our Values

  • Author Partnership: Building strong, collaborative relationships with authors.
  • Respect and Fairness: Treating authors with integrity and providing equitable terms.
  • Support and Guidance: Offering comprehensive support throughout the publishing process.
  • Nurturing Creativity:Fostering a creative environment where authors can thrive.
  • Amplifying Voices: Giving platform to diverse and underrepresented authors.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER & CEO

Kibirige Desire Edward is not just the founder and CEO of Desha Publishing House; he is a literary force, a Ugandan author whose passion for storytelling has driven him to create a platform for others. Widely recognized for his evocative poetry, published in acclaimed collections like "Bard Diaries" (2024) and "The Pearl" (2023), Kibirige's creative range extends beyond verse.

Background

Born in Busia district, Kibirige's journey began with a solid educational foundation. He pursued a Bachelor of Laws at the Islamic University in Uganda, but his heart remained rooted in literature.

Mission & Vision

The name "Desha Publishing House" stands for "Desire Edward Shares His Abilities," encapsulating his vision to empower writers. His goal is to create a platform where authors can thrive and share their voices with the world.
